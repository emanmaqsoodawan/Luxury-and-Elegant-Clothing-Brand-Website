Shipping & Returns

Premium service for premium clothing.

Complimentary Global Shipping

At E L E G A N C E, we believe the luxury experience extends to how your items are delivered. We offer complimentary express shipping worldwide on all orders. Your garments will arrive beautifully packaged in our signature boxes within 3-5 business days.

Return Policy

If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery. The item must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached. To initiate a return, simply log into your account or contact our concierge team.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer seamless and free exchanges. Please ensure the item meets our return conditions, and we will promptly ship out your replacement as soon as we receive your original item.
